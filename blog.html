Thirty Countries In Three Hundred Days ⤱


We are a travelling agency called GLOBE TROTTERS ⤱ and we use this blog to document our exprience of travelling to different countries, we offer affordable international travel packages and we give you tips you might find handy when travelling. We've been to five countries so far and we plan on travelling to 30 more in 2024. You can read our experience below.


1.The Beautiful Bali In Indonesia


January 2020 had to be the best month of our live's when we decided to travel to Bali. This trip was planned for about 6 months and that's the best thing we did to make our trip perfect. It was months of preparing, saving, going back and forth with airlines and hotels and getting our summer bodies in order for our first international trip. The trip consisted of us going to a temple at 5am, walking to the stunning beach a thousand times, meeting the kindest people ever from around the world, going to different waterfalls and scenic resorts and catching the most beautiful sunsets.



2. Thrilling Thailand


Here you'll find shocking cliffs jutting out of the ocean, giant flowers floating on crystal clear waters, and temples nestled in jungles on top of mountains, glittering with gold. The country's street food is just as incredible. We really loved being in Thailand. The buzzy nightlife in Bangkok had us so full of joy, we felt so safe and at peace.



3. Mountanious Switzerland


With it's breathtaking mountain views slightly covered in snow, might be the best country we've been to. Being on the train and seeing the beautiful mountains up close felt like something out of a fairy-tale movie, we could not believe it. Eternally grateful for having travelled to Switzerland, we went at a time when it was still snowing. Booked the most gorgeous cabin and we learned how to ski just outside the cabin, experience of a lifetime. We haven't worn our skii gear since then and I must say we have a reason to go back skiing in Switzerland or maybe in Vancouver during ski season.



4.Tulum Memories in México


Our trip to Tulum México was so special that we had to learn spanish months before we went. We figured this would help us communicate with the locals and this was very helpful as most only spoke Spanish. Tulum felt like home, that leaving after TWO whole weeks was so sad for us. The Mexican cuisine, the culture, amazing historical sites, turquiose beaches and the scenerey was all so great I encourage you to go explore the beautiful country of Mexico. Going back for Cancún next year.



5.Christ the Redeemer in Brazil


We went to Brazil for 5 nights and the main reason we went was to go see the Christ de Redeemer Statue in Rio, Brazil. The views below when we got to the statue were out of this world, so stunning that it feels like I saw them just yesterday. We didn't think we'd get to do anything but see the statue when we got to Rio but we were in for a surprise for those five days. We noticed how much football was loved in Rio, every other corner you would see people playing. Highlight of the trip had to be joining in on a friendly football match and that's the most fun I've had in so long. As grown as I am, it felt like Project Mbappe was in full effect.
